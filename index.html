Nicolas Yan

I am a Senior at Harvard College studying Social Studies with a research focus on Technology and the Future of Work. I am a coxswain on the Men's Heavyweight Crew team and a peer tutor at the Harvard College Writing Center.

I was born in Palo Alto, California (which I am quick to point out when the integrity of my support for Bay Area sports teams is brought into question) but I currently live in Auckland, New Zealand. In my free time, I enjoy going on ski trips, listening to music, thrift shopping, watching sport, and parking up with a good book.
